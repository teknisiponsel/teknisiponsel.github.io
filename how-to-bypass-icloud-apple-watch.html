20. how to make a glass of apple juice!


1Start by washing and then coring the apple to remove seeds. Cut the apples into slices. There is no need to peel the apples.2Add the apples to the pot and add enough water to just cover them. Too much water and you’ll have pretty diluted juice. This juice may come out a bit strong, but it’s a lot easier to dilute the juice with extra water rather than trying to make the flavor stronger.3Slowly boil the apples for about 20-25 minutes or until the apples are quite soft. Place a coffee filter or piece of cheesecloth in your fine mesh strainer and place over a bowl.4Slowly ladle the hot juice/apple mixture into a fine mesh strainer and gently mash the apples. The juice will be filtered through the bottom into your bowl while the apple mush will be left behind. Place the mush in a separate bowl for later. Repeat this process until all of your juice is in the bowl.5Taste the juice after it’s cooled for a bit. You can add additional sugar or cinnamon depending on your preferences. Again, if the flavor is too strong, you can add water a little bit at a time to weaken the flavor.6The apple mush you collected can easily be turned into applesauce by pureeing and adding a smidgen of sugar and cinnamon to taste.7Keep in mind your homemade apple juice doesn’t have any preservatives so be sure to keep it refrigerated and to use it within a week!Ingredients

18 Apples

Cinnamon (optional)

Sugar (optional)


Steps

1. Start by washing and then coring the apple to remove seeds. Cut the apples into slices. There is no need to peel the apples.

2. Add the apples to the pot and add enough water to just cover them. Too much water and you’ll have pretty diluted juice. This juice may come out a bit strong, but it’s a lot easier to dilute the juice with extra water rather than trying to make the flavor stronger.

3. Slowly boil the apples for about 20-25 minutes or until the apples are quite soft. Place a coffee filter or piece of cheesecloth in your fine mesh strainer and place over a bowl.

4. Slowly ladle the hot juice/apple mixture into a fine mesh strainer and gently mash the apples. The juice will be filtered through the bottom into your bowl while the apple mush will be left behind. Place the mush in a separate bowl for later. Repeat this process until all of your juice is in the bowl.

5. Taste the juice after it’s cooled for a bit. You can add additional sugar or cinnamon depending on your preferences. Again, if the flavor is too strong, you can add water a little bit at a time to weaken the flavor.

6. The apple mush you collected can easily be turned into applesauce by pureeing and adding a smidgen of sugar and cinnamon to taste.

7. Keep in mind your homemade apple juice doesn’t have any preservatives so be sure to keep it refrigerated and to use it within a week!

RESEP PUDING APEL

 

Apel memang kaya gizi. Dibandingkan dengan jeruk, apel mengandung 50% lebih banyak vitamin A. Jadi, tak ada salahnya, Anda olah buah yang satu ini menjadi makanan selingan untuk bayi usia 6-9 bulan.

Bahan:

3 buah (500 g) apel manis

1/2 sdt air jeruk manis

1 sdt air matang

1 sdm (10 g) tepung beras

1 sdt gula pasir

kayu manis bubuk untuk taburan (bila suka)

Cara membuat:

Kupas dan buang bagian tengah apel, lalu potong kecil-kecil. Masukkan ke dalam panci. Tambahkan air jeruk manis dan air, lalu masak di atas api sedang selama kurang 20 menit, sampai apel lunak.

Haluskan apel sampai menjadi bubur.

Campur dengan tepung beras dan gula. Aduk hingga rata.

Tuang ke dalam mangkuk tahan panas (@200 ml, diameter 8 cm), hingga batas 1 cm dari bibir mangkuk.

Panaskan oven hingga 180 derajat Celcius.

Taruh mangkuk dalam loyang, lalu tuang air mendidih ke dalam loyang setinggi 2/3 mangkuk. Masukkan ke dalam oven dan panggang selama sekitar 30 menit, sampai matang. Perhatikan jangan sampai air dalam loyang mendidih (jika mulai mendidih, segera kecilkan api oven).

Setelah matang, angkat dan tunggu hingga agak dingin.

Suapkan pada bayi dengan sendok kecil.

32. Apa yang dimaksud dengan bypass?


Bypass secara umum bermakna "jalan pintas" atau "jalan elak". Istilah bypass terdapat dalam berbagai bidang, mulai dari konstruksi, teknologi informasi, hingga medis.

Penjelasan:

Istilah bypass merupaka istilah yang banyak dijumpai di berbagai bidang, misalkan di bidang konstruksi kiat mengenal jalan bypass, di bidang kedokteran kita mengenal operasi bypass jantung, sedangkan di bidang teknologi informasi kita mengenal (diantaranya) istilah bypass proxy.

Apapun bidangnya, istilah bypass selalu memiliki makna yang sama, yaitu "jalan pintas" atau "jalan elak". Pada bidang konstruksi, jalan bypass bermakna jalan pintas (makna sebenarnya), pada bidang kedokteran bypass jantung berarti operasi dimana pembuluh darah lain digunakan sebagai jalan pintas darah untuk masuk ke jantung. Sedangkan istilah bypass proxy merujuk pada sejumlah alamat yang diberi pengecualian untuk dapat diakses tanpa melalu proxy ("proxy-nya dilewat/di-bypass). Kita dapat mengatur pengecualian ini pada bagian setting di browser yang kita pakai.
