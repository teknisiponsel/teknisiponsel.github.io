Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Indirect Speech / Reported Speech (kalimat tidak langsung) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Reported Speech / Indirect Speech, dalam bahasa Indonesia artinya adalah kalimat tidak langsung. Perubahan kalimat dari Direct Speech menjadi Reported Speech memiliki beberapa aturan:

1) Imperative Speech:

My mother said, "don't open that door!"

My mother said not to open that door.

Imperative: “Come here.” Reported Speech: She told me to come here. Jadi, kita memakai “ask/tell + to + verb” untuk melaporkan sebuah perintah atau imperative (kalimat perintah).

2) Present Tense:

The boy said, "I have a new apartment."

The boy said that he had a new apartment.

She said, "She is a dancer."

She said (that) She was a dancer.

Jika kalimat ini berbentuk kebenaran umum, maka akan lebih sesuai

She said (that) She is a dancer.

3) Past Tense:

My mother said, "You brother did his homework last night."

My mother said (that) my brother had done his homework previous night.

My friend said, "I was late."

My friend said that she had been late.

38. Mohon bantuannya please give me 3 sentences of direct speech in the daily activity? please give 3 sentences of direct speech in Nursing term (anything)?


PART = DIRECT SPEECH VS INDIRECT SPEECH

-----------------------

Jawaban:

DIRECT SPEECH IN THE DAILY ACTIVITY

1. The police officer said, "let me see your driver's license, please."

2. Mrs. Dee asked , " do you understand about the direct speech?"

3. Mr. Duncan said , " I am going to Bali next week."

DIRECT SPEECH IN NURSING TERM

1. " You must drink much water to keep your bladder healthy," doctor said.

2. The obstetrician said, " you can take ultrogestan once daily."

3. She asked , " where is my stethoscope?"

Penjelasan:

REPORTED SPEECH/INDIRECT SPEECHDirect speech ( kalimat langsung ) merupakan kalimat yang diucapkan langsung oleh si pembicara utama. Pada umumnya penulisannya terdapat tanda petik-nya /Quotation marks ( "......" )Sedangkan indirect speech/Reported speech ( kalimat tidak langsung ) merupakan pengucapan ulang pernyataan yang disampaikan oleh si pembicara utama ke orang lain di tempo waktu yang berbeda tanpa mengubah informasi nya.

Dalam mengubah kalimat langsung ( Direct speech ) harap diperhatikan beberapa hal berikut terkait perubahannya ke dalam bentuk kalimat tidak langsung ( indirect speech ) , yaitu :

1. Struktur kalimatnya , apakah berupa interrogative/affirmative/prohibition

Example :

First

"Don't open the door !"  ( direct speech )

She warned/prohibited not to open the door   ( indirect speech )

Analysis data  :

Struktur direct speech diatas berupa larangan ( prohibition/warning).Reporting verb "warned" mendefinisikan larangan / peringatanNoun clause yang digunakan menggunakan {not+to +base verb (verb 1)

---------

Second

Nina asked "where are you ?"    ( direct speech )

Nina wanted to know where i  was . ( indirect speech )

Analysis data :

Struktur direct speech diatas berupa kalimat tanya bentuk Wh-questionReporting verb ( wanted ) menggunakan pola simple past tense, struktur noun clause akan berubah menjadi bukan bentuk pertanyaan kembali tetapi bentuk pernyataan dimana to be ( are ) yang diubah  menjadi "was" karena mengikuti bentuk subyek

2.Tenses- nya dan struktur dari reporting verb nya ( present/past tense ) , karena pada umumnya , perubahan noun clause  mengikuti bentuk reporting verb-nya ( says , said ). Hal ini dikecualikan ketika pembahasan mengenai fenomena (fakta kebenaran umum) meskipun bentuk reporting verb nya bentuk simple past, tetapi struktur noun clause -nya tetap berbentuk simple present ( tidak ada perubahan ).

Example :

"The earth is round." said Mrs. Dee.    ( Direct speech )

Mrs. Dee explained to her students (that) the earth is round. ( indirect speech )

Analysis Data :

Karena yang dibahas mengenai fenomena, maka informasi yang diberikan tetap sama tanpa adanya perubahan bunyi tenses -nya meskipun reporting verb-nya bentuk simple past "explained."

3. Pronoun nya ( kata ganti orang )

Penerapan kata ganti orang akan berubah seiring posisi siapa yang mengutarakan pernyataan (berbicara) dan orang-orang yang dibicarakan.

4. Keterangan waktunya ( adverb of time )

Penggunaan adverb of time menyesuaikan waktu disaat percakapan tersebut muncul.

Example :

She said, " i am watching a movie now"   ( Direct speech )

She said she was watching a movie at that time.    ( indirect speech )
